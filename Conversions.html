Converting Degrees and Radians

This lesson will talk about how to convert degrees to radians and radical to degrees.

Converstions

In order to convert radians and degrees, you need to follow the below equations


Degree ---> Radian

You first place the angle/degree where the "x" is and place the degree next to the pi

You then simplify the numerator and demoninator if possible and you leave the pi alone

Once you do that you have converted the degree to a radian
